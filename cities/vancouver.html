Local tips for Vancouver
🚇
Use Main Street-Science World SkyTrain station
The Stadium-Chinatown station is closed during World Cup match days. Use Main Street-Science World station on the Expo Line instead. It is a short walk to BC Place via the Last Mile fan walk. SkyTrain runs every 2 minutes on match days.
✈️
Canada Line from YVR Airport to downtown
Vancouver International Airport (YVR) has a direct SkyTrain Canada Line to downtown in 26 minutes. Extremely easy arrival. No need for taxis or transfers if you stay downtown.
🍁
No US visa needed for Vancouver only
If attending matches only in Vancouver and Toronto, you do not need a US visa. A Canadian eTA or visitor visa is all you need. This is a major advantage and cost saving compared to the US host cities.
🌧️
Pack for rain even in June
Vancouver in June can be wet and cool especially evenings. BC Place has a retractable roof so matches are protected from rain. But getting to and from the stadium may involve wet weather. Bring a light waterproof jacket.
🏔️
One of the most beautiful host cities
Vancouver is surrounded by mountains and ocean. Build in extra time to explore Stanley Park, Granville Island, the seawall, Gastown and North Shore mountains. Easily one of the best city experiences at the entire 2026 World Cup.
🎉
Fan Festival at PNE Hastings Park
The official FIFA Fan Festival is at PNE Hastings Park in East Vancouver. Free entry, live match broadcasts, food and cultural events throughout the tournament.

🛂
Canada entry requirements

Canada has no special World Cup visa. Requirements depend on your nationality. No US visa needed if only visiting Vancouver and Toronto.

📱
eTA — CAD $7 (most visitors)
UK, EU, Australia, Japan, UAE, Qatar and many others. Apply online at canada.ca. Usually approved in minutes.
📋
Visitor visa required
India, China, Pakistan, Nigeria and others. Apply through IRCC. Allow 2-8 weeks. Apply early.

Getting to BC Place
🚇
SkyTrain to Main Street-Science World
Expo Line to Main Street-Science World then Last Mile fan walk to BC Place. Stadium-Chinatown station is closed on match days. Runs every 2 minutes.
🚶
Walk from Yaletown
5-10 minute walk from Yaletown neighbourhood. Most convenient option if staying nearby.
🚗
Avoid driving on match days
Streets near BC Place close to vehicles on match days. SkyTrain is by far the best option. Rideshare surge pricing will be high after matches.
