Local tips for Toronto
🚋
TTC streetcar 509 or 511 is easiest to the stadium
From downtown hotels take the 509 Harbourfront or 511 Bathurst streetcar directly to Exhibition Loop, steps from BMO Field. Journey is around 12 minutes from downtown. Buy a Presto card or pay by contactless card.
🚆
GO Transit to Exhibition Station
From Union Station take GO Transit Lakeshore line to Exhibition GO Station, directly adjacent to the stadium and Fort York Fan Festival. Very convenient if staying near Union Station.
✈️
UP Express from the airport to downtown
Toronto Pearson Airport to Union Station in 28 minutes via the UP Express. Buy tickets on the UP Express app. Much faster and cheaper than taxi or rideshare from the airport.
🍁
No US visa needed for Toronto only
If you are only attending matches in Toronto and Vancouver you do not need a US visa. Most international visitors need a Canadian eTA or visitor visa. Check our Visa Guide for your passport requirements. This is a major advantage of Toronto over the US host cities.
🌡️
Warm but not extreme
Toronto in June is warm at around 20-26°C. Much more comfortable than the US southern cities. BMO Field is an open air stadium. Light layers for evening matches as it cools down after sunset.
🎉
Fan Festival at Fort York and The Bentway
The official FIFA Fan Festival runs at Fort York National Historic Site and The Bentway adjacent to Exhibition Place, a short walk from BMO Field. Free admission with advance registration. Live match broadcasts, food, music and cultural events throughout the tournament.
💰
Canada uses Canadian dollars
CAD is worth less than USD so prices feel more affordable for many international visitors. Tipping 15-20% is standard at restaurants. Contactless payment is accepted almost everywhere.

Canada entry requirements

Canada has no special World Cup visa. Entry requirements depend on your nationality. You need either an eTA or a visitor visa — not both.

📱
eTA — CAD $7 (most visitors)
UK, EU, Australia, Japan, UAE, Qatar and many others. Apply online at canada.ca. Usually approved in minutes. Required for air travel only.
📋
Visitor visa required
India, China, Pakistan, Nigeria and others. Apply through IRCC online portal. Allow 2-8 weeks processing time. Apply early.
