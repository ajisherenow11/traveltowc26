About TravelToWC26

Everything you need to know about this site in plain language.

What is TravelToWC26
TravelToWC26 is a free travel planning tool built for football fans attending the FIFA World Cup 2026. We cover visa requirements, flights, hotels, eSIM, insurance, transfers and official merchandise across all 16 host cities in the USA, Canada and Mexico. No account needed. No data collected.

We do not collect personal data. There is no account system, no login, no email signup and no tracking sold to third parties. The country and city you select on the homepage is used only to show you relevant information and is not stored anywhere. What you do on this site stays on this site.
