Local tips for Los Angeles
🚇
Metro K Line is your best route to the stadium
Take the Metro K Line (Crenshaw/LAX) to Hawthorne/Lennox or Crenshaw stations then board the free match day shuttle to SoFi. This is the only reliable way to avoid the gridlock around the stadium. Leave 2.5 to 3 hours before kickoff.
🚗
LA traffic is notorious — plan ahead
Los Angeles traffic is some of the worst in the world. On match days roads around SoFi Stadium are closed to non-residents. Do not attempt to drive. Pre-book parking if you must via ParkWhiz but expect very long delays.
🌞
Weather is warm but not extreme
LA in June and July is warm and sunny at around 25-30°C. SoFi Stadium has a partial roof cover but most of the open areas outside are exposed. Bring sunscreen and water.
🎉
Fan Festival at LA Memorial Coliseum
The official FIFA Fan Festival is at the historic Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum in Exposition Park. Live match broadcasts, concerts and food. Accessible via Metro E Line. Fan zones also at Venice Beach, Burbank, Union Station and West Harbor.
💵
Tip 18-20% everywhere
Tipping is expected at restaurants, bars, taxis and for most services in LA. Many places add a suggested tip to your bill automatically.
🎬
Make time for the city
LA is a world city beyond football. Hollywood, Santa Monica beach, Griffith Observatory, Korean BBQ in Koreatown, Grand Central Market downtown. Build in at least one full non-match day to explore.

Getting to SoFi Stadium
🚇
Metro K Line and free shuttle (recommended)
Take Metro K Line to Hawthorne/Lennox or Crenshaw station. Free match day shuttle runs direct to stadium gates.
🚶
Walk from Inglewood hotels
If staying in Inglewood near the stadium you can walk or use a rental bike. Dedicated bike racks available at the stadium.
🚗
Rideshare drop-off
Designated rideshare zones on Kareem Court and Pincay Drive. Expect surge pricing after matches. Book your return in advance.
